Xfce4-popup-whiskermenu (Alias "The Superkey")

The Whiskermenu under the "SuperKey" is not so hard to get back
  • Open the Whiskermenu and find ""keyboard""
  • Selcect "Applications Shortcuts" and klik the "+ Add" button.
  • Type the command "xfce4-popup-whiskermenu" and klik "OK"
  • Then choose the key layout you want, I have mine under "Ctrl+Super"
  • Then close the window
