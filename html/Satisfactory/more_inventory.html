If you need to add more inventory slots.

Without mods this is the best way:

Close your game, find your game.ini file (%LOCALAPPDATA%\FactoryGame\Saved\Config\Windows), open it and put there these 3 lines.
Save, start game and you will have 200 slots.

↓ Click to Copy ↓
[/Script/FactoryGame.FGPlayerSettings] mDefaultPlayerInventorySlots=200 mDefaultArmEquipmentSlots=5
