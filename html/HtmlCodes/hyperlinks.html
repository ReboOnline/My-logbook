How to link with #ID on the same page:

First:
make a "id word" to the link to like below <h2 id="Life">The World of Rebo tips for Life <span style='color: var(--red);'>Read this first</span></h2> Then:
make the link as below in your own text <a href="#Life"><span style='color: var(--red);'>Read me first</span></a>

How to link with #ID to another page

Just make a small adjustment to a normal link <a href="../../index.html#Life"><span style='color: var(--red);'>Read me first</span></a>
