ATS & ETS2 | Cheat-codes


This guide was created to make it easier for me to find cheat codes.
Here I collected almost all the codes that might come in handy.


Turning on the console

First, to start the console, you need to go to this directory:
..\Documents\American Truck Simulator

NOTE: Or to the folder where you installed your truck simulator if you changed it yourself.


Find the"config.cfg"

Find the "config.cfg" in de folder "American Truck Simulator" open it and find the values "uset g_developer" and "uset g_console"
Next, change the value from "0" to "1" - this will enable developer mode and console.
Like so uset g_console "1" and uset developer "1"
After changing, save the file and close it.

(A good program for this task is Notpad++)


Start the game
and use the tilde "~" to open the console.


Some Cheat-Codes

Time
g_set_time 5 will set the time to 5AM
Any value between 0 and 23 can be used, but keep in mind that time always goes forward and therefore your ride time is shortened


Teleportation
goto (city name) will teleport to any city.
Note that you spawn in the air, with numpad 8 you fly forward and with your mouse you can steer where you fly.
When you are down, place your truck back on the road with "CTRL F9", so make sure there is enough space and you are not too high.

Free camera
With the normal "0" on your keyboard you can get out of your truck and fly around as described above.
This is useful to view the dropoff site and then jump back into your truck with "1" or if you are hopelessly stuck you can move your truck to another location with "CTRL F9"

Traffic
g_traffic (0-10) - enable / disable / or change traffic density.
For example:

g_traffic 0 - removes traffic from the game.
g_traffic 1 - standard traffic.
g_traffic 10 - enable the densest traffic.
